(also if you want your account/data deleted please make a request in the discord server or contact us.

  • Device Information: Details such as your operating system, device model, and unique device identifiers.

1.2 User-Provided Information

  • Account Information: When you log in using PlayFab, we collect account details like your username and email address.

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share certain data with trusted partners to provide game services:

4. Audio Permissions

Olly Tag requests microphone access to enable voice chat in multiplayer sessions. Audio is processed locally and transmitted temporarily to other players. We do not record, store, or share your audio data.
